Is it a working website?

This website was created just as a personal project for a portfolio. There is no wish to commercialize it. It works but for demo purposes only. Be aware that it could stop working at any moment and that the design (website/email) is of course not optimal.

What is a limit price?

the limit price is an amount from which we are ready to buy the product. As soon as the price of the product goes below this limit, a notification is sent.

When do we receive email notification?

If the product price is lower than your limit price (the system checks this once a day), an email (an ugly email from a gmail account) is sent.

Is my limit price updated if a better price has been found?

Yes, whenever a better price is found, the limit price is updated with the new price.
